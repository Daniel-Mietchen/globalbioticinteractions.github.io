GloBI integrates with many existing external datasets. As datasets are independently managed, GloBI continually discovers and revisits data sources to include recent updates or changes. This page gives you some insight into which datasets are indexed, where they are located, and what their status is. If you are interested to learn about dataset management, please go here, or perhaps read the blog post "Life stages of Species Interaction Datasets".

Please submit an issue for suggestions or bugs.
